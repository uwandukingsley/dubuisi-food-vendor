about us

foodExpress hospitality limited

We are a leading food vendor and delivery platform, dedicated to providing delicious meals conveniently to your doorstep. With our passion for great food and commitment to exceptional service, we aim to satisfy your cravings and make dining a delightful experience.


At our food vendor and delivery website, we have carefully curated a diverse menu, featuring a wide range of cuisines and flavors. From hearty comfort food to exotic international dishes, we have something to suit every palate and dietary preference. Our talented team of chefs and culinary experts work tirelessly to ensure that each dish is prepared with the finest ingredients, bursting with flavor and freshness.


Convenience is at the heart of our service. Through our user-friendly website and mobile app, you can effortlessly browse our menu, place your order, and have it delivered straight to your door. We have partnered with reliable delivery providers to ensure prompt and secure delivery, so you can enjoy your meal hassle-free.

Our journey started in April 20, 2012. Incorperated as FoodExpress Hospitality Limited, our first branch opened in August 15, 2012, at Egbeda, Lagos. Since then, more have been opened, with more in the pipeline.


Customer satisfaction is our top priority. We value your feedback and constantly strive to improve our offerings and services. Our dedicated customer support team is always ready to assist you with any inquiries, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable ordering experience.

Whether you're looking for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or catering for a special event, we have you covered. Join us on our culinary journey and let us tantalize your taste buds with our delectable creations. Experience the convenience and delight of our food vendor and delivery website today.
